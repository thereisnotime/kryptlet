To add encrypted blobs, base64-encode your .age file and add it to values.yaml:

  blobs:
    myconfig.age: $(base64 -w0 myfile.age)

Then fetch a blob with your age private key:

  curl -H "Authorization: Bearer <AGE-SECRET-KEY-...>" \
    http://<host>/v1/blob/myconfig
